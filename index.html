About

Built on a foundation
most realtors don't have.

Vanessa Cantú isn't just a Realtor — she's a licensed General Contractor with over a decade of construction experience. Since 2014, she has been immersed in new construction, renovations, and the investment side of real estate. When she earned her Realtor license in 2020, she brought something most agents simply can't offer: the ability to see beyond the surface.

She understands the build process inside and out — from foundation to finish. Whether you're buying your first home, selling a property, investing in new construction, or evaluating renovation potential, Vanessa provides insight that goes far deeper than a standard market analysis.

Serving the Rio Grande Valley, she works with buyers, sellers, and investors who expect more from their Realtor. Bilingual in English and Spanish, she makes every client feel informed, confident, and well-represented.
